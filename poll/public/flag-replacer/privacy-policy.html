Privacy Policy

Syrian Flag Replacer is a Chrome extension designed to replace the Syrian flag emoji with a new version on Twitter and X.com.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing activity. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Permissions

The extension only requests access to Twitter and X.com in order to replace the flag emoji. No other sites or data are accessed.

Third-Party Services

No third-party services are used. No data is shared with anyone.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
